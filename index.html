How has the representation of MBTI Personality Types in movies changed over time, and how has movie success evolved over that same period?

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) is a personality test that categorizes individuals into one of 16 personality types. To read more about MBTI personalities, visit to www.16personalities.com. In this project, we will explore the representation of MBTI personality types in movies and how movie success has evolved over time. By selecting a personality category from the table or bar chart, you can see an area chart on the right that shows the distribution of personalities in that category over time. Selecting a specific personality type from the area chart will then generate a scatter plot that shows all movies with a character of that personality type and the movie's success measured by ROI. Hovering over a point in the scatter plot will display the movie's title, the role, ROI, and rating.
